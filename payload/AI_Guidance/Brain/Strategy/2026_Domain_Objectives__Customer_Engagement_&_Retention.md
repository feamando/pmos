 Customer Engagement & Retention Domain 2026
For 2026, our strategy is built on three parallel workstreams. Our foundational priority is to perfect a meal selection journey for our upcoming 500-meal menu that feels intuitive and personally curated for every customer. Alongside this, we will transform our growth model by attracting a massive, engaged user base to our app with compelling, free-to-use content and tools. A modernized loyalty program will unify these efforts, rewarding engagement across our entire ecosystem to fuel a more sustainable, customer-centric growth engine.
